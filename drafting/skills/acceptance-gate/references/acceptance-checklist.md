# Acceptance Checklist

## Spec fit

- Source scope, blueprint, review findings, and change notes were re-read.
- Every acceptance criterion has pass/fail/deferred/blocked status.
- Non-goals were not implemented accidentally.
- Scope or contract drift has a change note.

## Format fit

Load the relevant reference:
- `format-software.md`
- `format-report.md`
- `format-plan.md`
- `format-investigation.md`

## Evidence

- Verification is fresh enough for the claim.
- Output was inspected, not assumed.
- Failures and warnings are recorded.
- User overrides are recorded.

## Decision

Use exactly one: delivered, partial, blocked, needs-review, rolled-back.
