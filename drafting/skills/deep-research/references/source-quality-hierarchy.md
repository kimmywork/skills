# Source Quality Hierarchy

Use this hierarchy to grade evidence.

| Level | Source type | Typical use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Primary artifact, official data, source code, direct logs, legal text | strongest factual basis |
| 2 | Peer-reviewed or formally reviewed source | scholarly or technical claims |
| 3 | Official documentation or standards | product, protocol, compliance claims |
| 4 | Reputable expert analysis | interpretation and context |
| 5 | Community discussion, forum, anecdote | leads only |
| 6 | Model inference or unsourced summary | never sufficient alone |

Grade every important source for authority, recency, independence, relevance, and conflict risk.
